ABOUT

KG Reddy College of Engineering and Technology (KGRCET) is a premier institution dedicated to fostering innovation, academic excellence, and professional growth. Our vision is to empower students with industry-relevant skills, ensuring their success in the ever-evolving technological world.

Academic Excellence

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and technology. Our curriculum includes hands-on learning, research-driven projects, and industry collaborations.

Faculty & Learning Environment

Our experienced faculty members promote continuous learning, supported by modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and digital libraries.

Placements & Industry Tie-ups

We partner with leading companies to provide students with internships, training, and placement opportunities in top organizations.

Student Life & Extracurricular Activities

KGRCET offers a vibrant campus life with various clubs, cultural events, and technical competitions, fostering holistic development.
